Public Law

Public law regulates relationships between the state and individuals. This includes criminal law, administrative law, and constitutional law, ensuring governance is lawful and fair.

Private Law (Civil Law)

Civil law governs relationships between individuals, including contracts, torts, and property. It provides mechanisms to resolve disputes and compensate for harm.

Court Procedures & Legal Personnel

Criminal and civil procedures differ, with roles for judges, juries, and lawyers. Legal personnel ensure justice is delivered according to procedural rules.

Common vs Civil Law Systems

Common law relies on precedent and judge-made law; civil law relies on codified statutes. Understanding both systems helps compare international legal frameworks.
